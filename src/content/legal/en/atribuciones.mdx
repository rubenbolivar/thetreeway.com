This site is built on other people's work. Acknowledging what a system is made of is part of how we understand architecture: with traceability and no black boxes.

## Platform and development

- **Next.js** and **React** — framework and UI library.
- **TypeScript** — static typing across the codebase.
- **Tailwind CSS** — styling system.
- **next-intl** — internationalization (Spanish and English).
- **MDX** (next-mdx-remote, gray-matter) — editorial content for cases, insights, and legal pages.

## Services

- **Resend** — delivery of contact-form messages.
- **Cal.com** — scheduling of the diagnostic conversation.
- **Plausible Analytics** — aggregate, cookieless, privacy-respecting usage measurement.

## Typefaces

- **Source Serif 4** — display typeface, under the SIL Open Font License.
- **Inter** — body typeface, under the SIL Open Font License.
- **JetBrains Mono** — monospaced typeface for accents and metadata, under the SIL Open Font License.

All three are served from the site's own infrastructure, with no third-party calls.

## Thanks

To the open-source community that maintains these tools. Without that work, a boutique firm could not operate at this level of technical quality.
